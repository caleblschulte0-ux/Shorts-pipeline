## Description of Service

The Shorts Pipeline is a personal automation that:

1. Discovers trending news topics from publicly available RSS feeds
   and APIs.
2. Generates short-form explainer videos (≤60 seconds) combining
   synthesized narration, royalty-free stock footage, public-domain
   gameplay footage, and images from Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons.
3. Publishes the resulting videos to the operator's connected social
   media accounts (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook
   Reels) on a scheduled basis.

The service is operated by an individual for personal content
creation. It is not a multi-user platform.
